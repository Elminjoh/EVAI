AI CHATBOT PLATFORM
================================================================================

What does the AI chatbot do?
----------------------------
Our AI chatbot handles customer conversations autonomously using GPT-powered natural language understanding. It can:
  • Answer customer questions 24/7
  • Handle bookings, cancellations, and rescheduling
  • Process payments via Stripe integration
  • Escalate to human agents when needed
  • Work across multiple channels (website, SMS, WhatsApp, email)


How smart is the chatbot?
-------------------------
The chatbot uses advanced GPT technology to understand natural language, context, and intent. It can handle complex, multi-turn conversations and learns from your business information to provide accurate, relevant responses.


Can the chatbot take bookings?
------------------------------
Yes! The chatbot includes a built-in automated booking system with real-time availability. Customers can book appointments, reschedule, or cancel — all through natural conversation.


What channels does the chatbot work on?
---------------------------------------
The chatbot supports multiple channels:
  • Website widget (white-label with your branding)
  • SMS
  • WhatsApp
  • Email


Can I customise the chatbot's appearance?
-----------------------------------------
Absolutely. We provide a white-label widget that you can customise with your own branding, colours, and styling to match your website.


Does the chatbot integrate with payment systems?
------------------------------------------------
Yes, the platform includes Stripe payment integration, allowing customers to pay for services directly through the chatbot.


Is there an analytics dashboard?
--------------------------------
Yes. You get a full analytics dashboard that tracks:
  • All conversations
  • Booking metrics
  • Revenue tracking
  • Customer insights
  • Performance reports


What happens if the chatbot can't answer a question?
----------------------------------------------------
The chatbot has smart escalation built in. When it encounters a question it can't confidently answer, it can escalate to a human agent or collect the customer's details for follow-up.

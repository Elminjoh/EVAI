FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work? +

You get full access to your chosen plan for 14 days — no credit card required. At the end of your trial, you can subscribe to continue or your account will be paused. No surprises.

Can I switch plans later? +

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Plan changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. No penalties.

What happens if I exceed my conversation limit? +

We'll notify you when you approach your limit. If you exceed it, your chatbot will continue working — we won't cut off your customers. We'll suggest an upgrade at the end of the month.

Do I need technical skills to set up? +

Not at all. Our onboarding wizard guides you step by step. To add the chatbot to your website, you just paste one line of code. If you need help, our support team is always available.

How do I accept payments from customers? +

We use Stripe Connect, which lets you connect your own Stripe account and receive payments directly. We take a small 2.5% platform fee on transactions — Stripe's standard processing fees also apply.

What about custom software projects? +

Custom projects are quoted individually based on scope and complexity. We start with a free discovery call, then provide a detailed proposal with fixed pricing. No hourly billing surprises.

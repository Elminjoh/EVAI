About ElmiViceAI

We're a technology company on a mission to make intelligent automation accessible to every business — from the UK to South Africa and beyond.

Our Journey

Our Story

ElmiViceAI was born from a simple observation: small and medium businesses were losing customers because they couldn't respond fast enough. Missed calls, unanswered website queries, and manual booking processes were costing them growth.

We set out to change that. Starting with AI chatbots that could handle real conversations, we expanded into automated booking systems, analytics platforms, and full custom software development. Today, we help businesses across the United Kingdom and South Africa convert more customers, save hours of manual work, and scale with confidence.
